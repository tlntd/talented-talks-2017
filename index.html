Talented welcomes all the members of the Talented Network and their plus ones* to Talented Talks – event with world class speakers and topics. We dive into the world's top companies and how they use technology in their businesses.

The keynote speakers include Lieke Boon from Github, Diego González-Zúñiga from Samsung WebVR, Patrik Weston from Google and Niko Kivelä from Finnair. The event will be held at the spectacular Tennispalatsi Scape Room. After the talks the evening continues at the Fraktio Office with the sweaty afterparty.

*If you have IT-wizard buddy who isn't part of the Talented Network but would also like to join, worry not! All he or she needs to do is to tell us your name when enrolling in.
